Welcome to **The Nice Data**. I am **Nguyễn Xuân Lưu**, the author of this blog.

**My Mission**

I believe that data is the most valuable asset of every modern business. My goal is to contribute solutions to help organizations build a solid, scalable, and highly secure data foundation, thereby unlocking hidden value through AI and Analytics.

**Areas of Expertise**

- **System Architecture (System Architect):** Designing distributed, high-load systems on both On-Premise and Cloud platforms.
- **Database Administration (DBA):** Optimizing performance and ensuring data security for RDBMS and NoSQL systems.
- **Data Engineering:** Building simple and efficient data pipelines (ETL/ELT).
- **Data Governance:** Simplifying and improving the enforceability of processes and standards to ensure data quality.
- **Artificial Intelligence (AI Engineering):** Applying AI models to real-world business problems.

**Why "The Nice Data"?**

This name reflects my working philosophy: Data should not only be "big" but also "nice." A "nice" dataset is clean, well-structured, easy to understand, and ready to generate value.

I hope the content shared on this blog will be helpful to you on your journey to mastering the world of data!

**About Me**

I am an engineer working in Hanoi, Vietnam. I am married and have a happy small family.

In 2015, I studied Electronic and Telecommunications Engineering at Hanoi University of Science and Technology. This is where I pursued my passion for science and technology. I was trained with professional knowledge, skills, and the mindset of an engineer in the technology field. Hanoi University of Science and Technology is where I have many great memories, friendships, and valuable relationships.

In 2020, I graduated with an excellent engineering degree and started working at VNPT VAS. This unit specializes in researching, developing, and operating value-added services on telecommunications platforms and digital services under VNPT Group. I started as a system monitoring staff member, working 24/7 shifts. Later, I took on the role of a system engineer, operating platform services in the telecommunications utility domain. At the same time, I pursued a master's program at Hanoi University of Science and Technology with a full scholarship from the university.

In 2022, I completed my master's degree with a good classification in Telecommunications Engineering. I fully shifted to applied engineering and stopped pursuing academic research. I continued working at VNPT VAS and took on additional responsibilities related to data, participating in building a data platform for business reporting.

In 2024, I specialized in the complex field of Oracle database administration. I completed the training process, passed the OCP certification exam, and worked with database systems using some of the most advanced technologies in the world. At the same time, I took a leading role in researching and implementing big data solutions within the enterprise.

In 2026, my team and I completed the development of a big data platform for the enterprise. The platform was built on a virtualized environment using open-source and free solutions, including key technologies such as Apache Airflow, Apache NiFi, Apache Spark, MinIO, SingleStore, and Metabase.
